Some (Brief) Things About Myself

I am a mechanical and aerospace engineer currently based in Southern Arizona. As an engineer, I aspire to work on design teams to innovate and push technology forward, with the goal of leading a team of my own one day. Throughout the years, I have enjoyed turning complex problems into simple, beautiful, and intuitive and easy designs. When I am not working on projects, I can be found playing musical instruments, pursuing a lifelong interest in the arts and training while keeping a healthy body.
